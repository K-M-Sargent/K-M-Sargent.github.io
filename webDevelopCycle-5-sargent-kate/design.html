Step 4: Design

What makes websites like Google.com and Youtube.com so popular? Well, aside from their uses and popularity in culture, it's their recognizable designs. They're known for certain colors and sizes and structures. You should always aim for recognizable imagery and acessible workings when designing a website.

What makes a website get more views?

  • Colors that match but aren't unreadable and don't hurt eyes
  • A cohesive theme
