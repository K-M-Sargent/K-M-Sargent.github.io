Step 1: Research

Basic steps of Research

  • Collect information from valuable resources
  • Put all information on a doc, note, list, etc.

In order to have a website with valuable information, you need to do research first. A common way of doing this without losing track of where you put your information is to use something like a Google Document. All sources you use should be fact-checked and resonsible.
