Step 3: Assembly

Before creating a website, you need to figure out some things, like: how much information you wish to include, what’s the most and least important information, and how to space out the information. In order to know some of these things, especially the lengths of the paragraphs and sizes of website information, we need to first write it out and assemble it.

"Why should I do this step?"

  • Gathering what you're going to write on your page and the general informaton helps you plan out how big or small of spaces you need for your paragraphs.
  • People can tell when you rush and don't make the writing (and check it) beforehand, and most viewers will choose not to trust a site seemingly made by someone irresponsible and/or unprepared.
