The Best Holidays

Top Holidays:

  • Halloween
  • Fourth of July
  • My Birthday
  • Thanksgiving
  • New Years

Halloween

During halloween, anyone can be anything. Whether someone dresses up as a spooky ghost, a nurse, or some niche character from a movie filmed in the 80's, every costume is unique and says things about every person! My family was never the type for themed group costumes, though I am doing those with a friend this year. I'm going as Hermes, and she's going as Apollo!

Fourth of July

Fireworks, slightly overcooked hotdogs drowned in ketchup, sprinklers, and family. I love the fourth of July for the same reason many people do: spending time with family, the beautiful skies, and the food.

My Birthday

Obviously the one holiday that should be celebrated around the world, my Birthday! I always have rollerskating parties on my birthdays, and I plan on keeping it that way. My birthday is also coming up as I'm making this (november 8th)!

Thanksgiving

"I wonder why Ell loves Thanksgiving," I hear you say through this computer screen, and of course it's because of the food! My personal favorite either has to be the mac n' cheese, or the mashed potatos and gravy, heavily based on the texture of the gravy.

New Years

While being right next to my least favorite holiday: Christmas, I love New Years! It's fun, casual, and you can watch the countdown til the next year arrives. I don't ever really get the hang of writing the new correct date on my assignments until around May, though.
